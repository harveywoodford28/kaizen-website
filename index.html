FAQ

Common questions.

Why start with a discovery instead of just building?+
AI without strategy creates waste. The discovery ensures every pound you spend goes toward solutions that actually save you time and generate return. We've seen businesses waste thousands building the wrong thing first.
Is my business too small for this?+
No. Some of our biggest impact has been with solo operators and teams under 10 people. In fact, smaller businesses often see faster ROI because the time savings are felt immediately. If you have customers, leads, or appointments — AI can help.
Do I have to commit to building anything?+
No. The discovery gives you a clear roadmap with costs and ROI projections. You decide what to action and when. Some clients adopt free tools we recommend and see immediate value before investing in custom systems.
Will AI replace my team?+
AI handles the repetitive work your team shouldn't be doing — answering phones, chasing leads, sending reminders. It frees your people to focus on what they're actually good at. It's about making your team more effective, not smaller.
How quickly can systems go live?+
Our delivery partners can have core systems configured and live within 48 hours of sign-off. Full CRM + AI receptionist deployments typically go live within a week.
